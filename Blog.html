Social media creates platforms for people to express, embrace and share stories. Such platforms such as twitter, facebook, Instagram and snap chat can easily spread ideas and cause millennials to make a difference around the globe. The #metoo movement started with Tarana Burke in 2006, who used the term “me too” to help women who suffered through sexual violence. The term has recently been renewed in the past ten years as a way for people to share their stories of sexual violence through social media. In 2017, Ashley Judd accused Harvey Weinstein of assaulting her in an article with The New York Times.
